About Mama Elimah

Mama Elimah Kasoha Amatalo, fondly remembered as Zelphata during her youth, was a devoted wife, loving mother, and committed servant of God. Her life was a testament to unwavering faith, community service, and boundless love for her family. She leaves behind a legacy of kindness and spiritual devotion that will inspire generations to come.
